About Machin Nursery Farms

Machin Nursery Farms is a family-owned nursery that has served Homestead, Florida since 2004. We focus on practical plant recommendations for local climate conditions and support both retail homeowners and professional landscape teams.

Our owner-led team has spent two decades helping customers choose plants that survive and perform, including hedging species, tropical ornamentals, and project-ready material for contractor timelines.

For bilingual customers, we maintain dedicated English and Spanish pages so visitors and crawlers can access clear information in both languages.
