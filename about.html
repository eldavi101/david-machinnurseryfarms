About Machin Nursery Farms

A family-owned tropical plant nursery serving South Florida since 2004, dedicated to helping homeowners and professionals find plants that thrive in local conditions.

Our Story and Mission

Machin Nursery Farms was founded in 2004 with a clear mission: to provide South Florida homeowners, landscapers, and contractors with high-quality tropical plants backed by genuine expertise. Our family recognized a gap in the local market—most nurseries either offered limited tropical varieties or lacked deep knowledge of how plants perform in Miami-Dade County's specific climate conditions. We started small, focused on building relationships with customers who understood that plant selection and care are investments in long-term landscape success.

Over two decades, we've grown from a small operation into a trusted resource for both residential and wholesale customers across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Monroe counties. But our core principle remains unchanged: every recommendation we make is based on decades of hands-on experience growing and caring for tropical plants in South Florida.

What Sets Our Nursery Apart

Unlike large-scale garden centers that stock generic inventory, we specialize in tropical and native plants specifically adapted to South Florida's subtropical climate. Our team has planted and maintained gardens throughout the region, so we understand exactly how each species behaves under local conditions—how fast it establishes, what moisture it needs, and what seasonal stresses it encounters. This isn't theoretical knowledge; it comes from years of direct field experience.

We also recognize that our customers have different needs. Homeowners need guidance on how to choose and care for plants in their yards. Contractors need reliable stock, efficient ordering, and coordination for project schedules. Landscaping professionals need access to specialty species and wholesale pricing. We've built our operation to serve all three customer types with equal attention to quality and reliability.
