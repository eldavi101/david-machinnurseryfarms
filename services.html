Our Nursery Services in South Florida

Complete plant and landscape solutions for homeowners, contractors, and landscaping professionals.

Local Plant Nursery Sales - Retail and Wholesale

We stock a curated selection of tropical plants, native Florida species, and ornamental trees adapted to South Florida conditions. Whether you're shopping for a single plant or planning a large landscape project, our team helps you select species that will thrive in your specific site conditions. We serve retail homeowners, contractors, and landscaping professionals with wholesale pricing on volume orders.

Professional Landscape Design and Consultation

Our experts provide landscape design services tailored to your site's sun exposure, soil conditions, and maintenance preferences. We recommend plant combinations that create visual appeal, structural integrity, and long-term performance. Whether you need a privacy hedge, a color-focused accent planting, or a complete garden redesign, we guide you through species selection and site preparation.

Plant Delivery and Professional Installation

We provide complete delivery and installation service throughout South Florida, including Miami-Dade, Broward, and Monroe counties. Our installation team ensures plants are correctly positioned, properly planted at the right depth, and established with appropriate irrigation. Professional installation improves plant survival rates and gets your landscape off to the right start.

Expert Plant Consultation and Care Guidance

Our team brings over two decades of hands-on experience growing and maintaining tropical plants in South Florida. We provide personalized guidance on plant selection, establishment care, seasonal maintenance, and troubleshooting for common plant stress issues. Whether you're new to tropical gardening or managing a large commercial landscape, we're available for consultation before and after purchase.

Wholesale and Contractor Support

We specialize in supporting landscape contractors and construction teams with project-specific inventory, coordination of delivery windows, and quantity pricing. Our wholesale location offers efficient ordering for recurring projects and can accommodate special orders for specific species and sizes. Contact our wholesale team for volume pricing and project planning.
