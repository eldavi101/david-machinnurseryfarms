Privacy Policy

How Machin Nursery Farms collects and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through direct communication channels including:

  • Email inquiries to info@machinnurseryfarms.com or wholesale@machinnurseryfarms.com
  • Phone calls to our business numbers
  • In-person visits to our retail location
  • Contact forms or requests on this website

Information collected may include your name, email address, phone number, and details about your plant or service request.

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information you provide for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiry or service request
  • Scheduling appointments or coordinating plant deliveries
  • Providing customer support and follow-up service
  • Communicating project details and pricing information
  • Improving our nursery services based on customer feedback

Data Security and Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We recommend not sharing sensitive financial information via email unless you are using a secure connection.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Information may be disclosed only when required by law or in connection with our service delivery (such as delivery or installation coordination).

Analytics and Website Data

We may use website analytics tools to collect aggregate information about site usage, including page views, referring sites, and general traffic patterns. This data is used to improve website functionality and user experience. Analytics data is typically anonymized and does not identify individual visitors unless you contact us directly.

Your Rights and Data Access

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information that you have provided to us. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@machinnurseryfarms.com with your request.

Policy Updates

Machin Nursery Farms may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable privacy laws. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for updates.
