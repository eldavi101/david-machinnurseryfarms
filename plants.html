Tropical Plants and Trees for South Florida

Browse our selection of plants, hedges, ornamental trees, and flowers adapted to Homestead and South Florida climate conditions.

Privacy and Screening Plants

Create natural privacy screens and windbreaks with our selection of dense, fast-growing hedging plants. Clusia, Podocarpus, and Cocoplum are popular choices that handle South Florida heat and humidity. Each species has different growth rates, mature sizes, and maintenance requirements. Our team helps you choose the right combination for your specific site conditions, sun exposure, and budget.

Tropical Accent and Color Plants

Add visual interest and tropical character with vibrant foliage and flowering plants. Crotons, tropical palms, and flowering specimens create focal points and landscape structure. We stock varieties with different color patterns, growth habits, and care requirements to match your landscape design and maintenance capabilities.

Native and Adapted Ornamental Trees

Native Florida species and adapted ornamental trees provide structure, shade, and environmental benefits. These species are proven to thrive in local soils and climate conditions, requiring less supplemental care once established. We recommend native options that support local wildlife while creating beautiful, functional landscapes.

Landscape Project Stock for Contractors

We maintain inventory specifically selected for common contractor and landscaping projects.
